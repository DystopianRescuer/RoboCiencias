enum Positions

Class used for easier handling of the robot's legs.

It has two modes depending on the quantity of legs: 1) Two legged mode: 2) Three legged mode:

The stances of combat are predefined positions for the legs that are used for specific cases. Also, they are the way of stopping the movement of the robot.

ATTACK stance: Inclined to the back and with a lower center of mass. The ideal stance for offensive actions NORMAL stance: With normal degrees and leg position. The ideal stance for walking actions
